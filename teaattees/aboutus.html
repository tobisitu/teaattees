The Story behind Tea at Tee's

The Founder

Tea at Tee’s Cafe is owned by Ms. Tiwa Osubare- a young Nigerian Immigrant to Canada.

Tiwa’s favourite things to do are to drink tea and travel and so she created a business that combined her hobbies and allowed her to share her tea-finds with people around her.

Tiwa believes hard work and discipline helped her get to where she is today and she wanted her story to be shared on her website to inspire others.

With encouragement from friends and family, she decided to take a chance, step out of her comfort zone and follow her dreams!

The StartUp

Tea at Tee’s Cafe is a fairly new Nigerian-Canadian Company that recently started growing in Ottawa, Canada.

One thing that makes this company stand out is “Tee’s Tea of the month” which has been going on since the company began in 2014. They feature a new/favourite tea from different parts of the world every month. In the last couple of months they went viral on Instagram and “Tee’s Tea of the month” has become something people look forward to monthly. This feature is very important to the company and is highlighted monthly on the website.

The Goal

Having been granted this opportunity to chase this dream, Tea at Tee’s aims to unite fellow tea lovers of all ages and backgrounds with authentic tea from around the world, and to provide a unique experience with each different tea.

And while doing this, Tiwa plans to inspire others to reach for the stars just like those in her life inspired and supported her.
